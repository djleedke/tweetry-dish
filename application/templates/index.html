    Welcome to @TweetryDish

    Greetings all! The idea behind this app is simple:

    • Each day a new famous quote is generated.
    • Users work together to create a "new" quote by voting to change individual words.
    • You can vote once per hour by clicking on each word of the quote.
    • You have a choice of selecting one of the existing top words, or you can find a word of your own.
    • At 8:00pm EST the quote will be tweeted using the top choice for each word that was voted for.
    • Rinse and repeat!

    ...and that's it! I hope you enjoy it, please try to keep things as clean as possible. ;) Also, be sure to follow @TweetryDish to see the finished quotes!

    Feedback is always appreciated so feel free to send me a message here. Also the code for this project is posted on Github, so feel free to take a look!
